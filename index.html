Little Baby Girl (Simmu) ❤️

You are precious — not because of how you look, but because of the heart you carry.
You deserve to be understood, not judged… valued, not compared… respected… and loved without fear.

I don’t give up on people I genuinely care about.
I don’t walk away when things get hard.
I won’t make you feel unwanted.
I won’t let you sleep with a heavy heart.
And I won’t kill the inner child inside you — the one that laughs freely, dreams openly, and deserves softness.

You deserve to be treated right — with effort, honesty, love, patience and respect.
And I truly mean it — I will put in the effort to prove it.
You are valued… you are wanted… and you matter. ❤️
